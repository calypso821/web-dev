High-rise glass buildings won’t look so good if they weren’t cleaned from outside from time to time. Cleaning glass of such high rise buildings is very tricky and dangerous job. This involves workers hanging on to the outer side of the building using ropes and harnesses while cleaning the glass. There a wide number of deaths due to falling during glass cleaning activities.
Well here we propose a smart lass cleaner robot that can climb glass from outside using vacuum creation to climb and clean glass from outside. This Window & Glass Cleaning Robot also helps one reach & clean every corner of your glass or window where it is difficult for a human hand to reach.
